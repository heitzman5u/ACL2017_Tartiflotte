RPG 2D game.
The player handles a character seen from above, who can move freely in levels made of tiles.
The camera is fixed, and displays the entire level.
Monsters can hit the character, and vice-versa.
The game features a war fog, which restricts the player's view.


Functionnalities:

There are different tiles types{
	grass, wall, ladder
}

The character can move on 4 directions, with constant speed.
The character cannot move on walls.
The character has a life bar.
There are (identical) monsters on the level.
The monsters have life bars.
The monsters can attack the character with a 1-tile ranged attack every second.
The players loses a constant amount of life when hit.
When the player's life goes under 0, he dies, and "YOU DIED" appears on screen for a few seconds before restarting the level as on first launch.
The player can attack monsters by hitting the left, bottom, right, up buttons. A 1-tile ranged attack occurs.
The monster loses a constant amount of life when hit.
When the monster's life goes under 0, he dies, and disappears until the level restarts.
A fog of war is implemented{
	A box around the character's current tile is displayed, all other tiles are black.
	The box is updated when the character's center is above a new tile.
}
When the player reaches a ladder, a new level is loaded{
	The player spawns next to a ladder.
	When moving on this ladder, the previous level is loaded.
	The player and monsters keep their life between levels.
}
Levels are read from files.
